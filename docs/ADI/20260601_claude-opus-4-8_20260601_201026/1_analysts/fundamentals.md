## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Analog Devices, Inc. (ADI)** is a global leader in the design, manufacture, and marketing of high-performance analog, mixed-signal, and digital signal processing (DSP) integrated circuits. Founded in 1965 and headquartered in Wilmington, Massachusetts, ADI serves markets including industrial automation, automotive, communications, and healthcare. ADI's 2021 acquisition of Maxim Integrated significantly expanded its scale and product breadth, making it one of the world's largest analog semiconductor companies.
