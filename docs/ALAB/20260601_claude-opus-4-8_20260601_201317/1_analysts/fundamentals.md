## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Astera Labs, Inc. (ALAB)** is a fabless semiconductor company specializing in connectivity and cloud-optimized AI infrastructure. The company designs purpose-built semiconductor and software solutions for AI and cloud data centers, with a focus on intelligent connectivity using PCIe, CXL (Compute Express Link), and Ethernet fabric technologies. ALAB enables hyperscaler and cloud service providers to overcome critical bottlenecks in AI training and inference workloads. The company went public in March 2024, raising approximately $672 million in its IPO—one of the most notable semiconductor IPOs of the year.

**Key Products:** ARIES (PCIe/CXL retimers), TAURUS (Ethernet retimers), LEO (CXL Memory Controllers), SCORPIO (smart fabric switches).
