## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Business Overview

**Applied Materials, Inc. (AMAT)** is the world's largest semiconductor equipment manufacturer by revenue. Founded in 1967 and headquartered in Santa Clara, California, AMAT designs, manufactures, and services equipment, software, and services used in the fabrication of semiconductor chips, flat panel displays, and solar photovoltaic cells. Its three main business segments are:

- **Semiconductor Systems** – Deposition, etch, ion implant, lithography, and inspection equipment
- **Applied Global Services (AGS)** – Parts, services, training, and software for installed equipment base
- **Display & Adjacent Markets** – Equipment for LCD/OLED flat panels and flexible electronics

AMAT sits at the critical intersection of the AI/data center infrastructure build-out, advanced semiconductor logic scaling (3nm → 2nm → sub-2nm), and DRAM/NAND memory upgrades, making it a key beneficiary of multi-year secular tailwinds.
