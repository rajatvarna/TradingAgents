## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (AMD)** is a global semiconductor company headquartered in Santa Clara, California. AMD designs and develops high-performance computing and visualization products including CPUs (EPYC for data centers, Ryzen for consumer), GPUs (Instinct for AI/HPC, Radeon for gaming/consumer), FPGAs, and Adaptive SoCs (acquired via Xilinx in 2022). AMD competes directly with Intel (x86 CPUs), NVIDIA (GPUs/AI accelerators), and Qualcomm (ARM-based compute) across Data Center, Client, Gaming, and Embedded markets.

**Key Business Segments:**
- **Data Center**: EPYC server CPUs and Instinct AI/MI-series GPU accelerators
- **Client**: Ryzen consumer/commercial processors
- **Gaming**: Radeon GPUs and semi-custom SoC chips for consoles
- **Embedded**: FPGA/Adaptive SoC products from the Xilinx portfolio

7. **Customer Concentration**: Hyperscaler data center revenues are increasingly concentrated among a small number of major cloud providers.
8. **High Beta (2.40)**: AMD is highly susceptible to broad tech market sell-offs and sentiment shifts around AI spending.
