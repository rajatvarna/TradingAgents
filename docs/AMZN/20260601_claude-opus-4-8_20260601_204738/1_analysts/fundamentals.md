## 1. Company Profile & Overview

Amazon.com, Inc. (AMZN) is one of the world's largest technology and e-commerce conglomerates. Its business spans five major areas:
- **Online & Physical Retail** (first-party e-commerce, Amazon Fresh, Whole Foods)
- **Third-Party Marketplace** (seller services, logistics fulfillment)
- **Amazon Web Services (AWS)** (cloud infrastructure and platform services — the primary profit engine)
- **Advertising Services** (high-margin digital ad revenue)
- **Subscriptions** (Prime membership, streaming content, digital media)

As of June 1, 2026, AMZN carries a **market capitalization of ~$2.81 trillion**, making it one of the most valuable companies in the world.

2. **Debt Surge:** Long-term debt doubled in Q1 2026 (~$53B issuance). While manageable, the leverage increase is notable and adds financial risk in a rising rate environment.

6. **Macro/Cyclical Risk:** With Beta of 1.47, AMZN is highly sensitive to broader market downturns, interest rate movements, and consumer spending cycles.
