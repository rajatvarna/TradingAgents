## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Arista Networks, Inc. (ANET)** is a leading provider of cloud networking solutions for large data center environments, high-frequency trading, and enterprise campuses. Its flagship products include its EOS (Extensible Operating System) software platform and a broad portfolio of high-performance switches and routers engineered for the hyperscale cloud era. Key customers include major cloud titans (Meta, Microsoft), financial services firms, and enterprise clients. Arista competes primarily against Cisco, Juniper (now HPE Networking), and emerging players in the AI-driven networking space.

The company is headquartered in Santa Clara, California, and has been a primary beneficiary of the surge in AI/ML-driven data center infrastructure spending, which demands ultra-high bandwidth switching fabrics and low-latency connectivity.
