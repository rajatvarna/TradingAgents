When the bull case itself builds in a pullback expectation, the honest synthesis is: own the business, don't chase the price.

For anyone already holding ARM with a lower cost basis, the structural thesis is intact and validating; trimming into euphoria and holding the core is sensible. For new capital, paying 133x forward (200x on realistic near-term earnings) with every optimistic input required to fire simultaneously, into a parabolic technical setup, is a poor risk-adjusted entry. Neither "Buy more aggressively" nor "Sell/exit a great franchise" is warranted.
