And the "you'll never re-enter on a dip" behavioral problem the bear acknowledges is a real cost of the waiting strategy.

Net-net: this is a high-quality, structurally compounding asset where the long-term arguments clearly favor ownership, but the specific entry point at ATH on the eve of a binary catalyst argues against deploying full size today. That's a textbook Overweight, not a Buy and not a Hold. The trader should be building exposure with respect for the catalyst, not chasing or waiting on the sidelines.
