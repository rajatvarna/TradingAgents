## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

Hewlett Packard Enterprise Company (HPE) is a global technology leader that provides IT infrastructure, hybrid IT solutions, edge computing, intelligent edge networking, and financial services to enterprises. Spun off from HP Inc. in November 2015, HPE serves customers across more than 170 countries. The company is organized around key segments including Servers, Hybrid Cloud, Intelligent Edge (Aruba), HPE Financial Services, and the recently acquired Juniper Networks business.

The acquisition of **Juniper Networks** — completed in mid-FY2025 for approximately **$12.3 billion** — has been the most transformative corporate event in recent history, significantly reshaping HPE's balance sheet and revenue profile, adding networking capabilities that position HPE as a direct competitor to Cisco in enterprise networking.
