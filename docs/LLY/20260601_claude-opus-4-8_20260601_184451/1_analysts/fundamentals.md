## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

Eli Lilly and Company (`LLY`) is one of the world's leading pharmaceutical companies, headquartered in Indianapolis, Indiana. Founded in 1876, Lilly operates globally and focuses on discovering, developing, manufacturing, and marketing medicines in core therapeutic areas including **diabetes/obesity (GLP-1 agonists), oncology, immunology, and neuroscience**.

The company has experienced a transformational period from 2022 to 2026, primarily driven by the explosive commercial success of **Mounjaro (tirzepatide)** for diabetes and **Zepbound** for obesity — its GLP-1/GIP dual agonists — alongside strong performance from **Verzenio** (abemaciclib, breast cancer), **Taltz** (ixekizumab, immunology), and **Jardiance**.
