## 1. 🏢 Company Profile & Overview

**Lam Research Corporation** is a global leader in semiconductor equipment manufacturing, specifically focused on **wafer fabrication equipment** and related services. 7. **Geopolitical Risk**: Export controls and US-China trade tensions can materially impact Lam's access to Chinese customers, historically a large revenue contributor.
