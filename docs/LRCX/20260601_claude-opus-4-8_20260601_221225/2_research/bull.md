## 7. 🔬 The Bollinger "Band Walk Failure" — A Technical Misreading

A "band walk" in technical analysis refers to a pattern where price consistently rides along the Bollinger Upper Band for multiple sessions — producing closes *at or near* the upper band for an extended period. A "band walk failure" would be a breakdown after an extended period of upper band riding.
