This positions Marvell for a potential second major upcycle beyond the current generative AI boom.

## 4. ⚔️ Competitive Landscape

### MRVL vs. NVDA
Zacks analysts note that while both Nvidia and Marvell are riding the AI infrastructure boom, **Nvidia retains advantages** in:
- Faster overall revenue growth.
- Higher gross margins.
- Valuation (arguably more reasonable on earnings-based metrics).

However, Marvell is winning in a differentiated market — **custom silicon for hyperscalers** who want to reduce Nvidia dependency — giving it a distinct and defensible niche.

### Gross Margin Headwind (Key Risk)
Marvell's gross margin expansion has been **slower than expected**. Rising custom silicon production costs and competition are compressing margins. This is a key watch item for Q2 FY2027 results.
