## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

PDD Holdings Inc. is one of the world's largest e-commerce conglomerates, best known for operating **Pinduoduo** (a social commerce platform dominant in mainland China) and **Temu** (its rapidly growing international marketplace). Founded on a group-buying model, PDD has evolved into a multi-platform digital commerce powerhouse. The company is incorporated internationally and listed on the NASDAQ under the ticker **PDD**.

PDD competes directly with Alibaba (BABA), JD.com (JD), and Shopee in China and internationally. Its differentiated value proposition — deep discounts via manufacturer-direct selling and gamified social shopping — has driven extraordinary user acquisition and monetization growth.
