## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Palantir Technologies Inc. (PLTR)** is a leading American software company headquartered in Denver, Colorado, specializing in big data analytics, AI-powered decision platforms, and defense/intelligence software. Founded in 2003, Palantir serves both government and commercial clients globally.

**Core Products:**
- **Gotham** — Primary platform for U.S. government, defense, and intelligence agencies
- **Foundry** — Commercial data integration and AI analytics platform for enterprises
- **AIP (Artificial Intelligence Platform)** — AI and large-language-model orchestration across enterprise workflows
- **Apollo** — Continuous deployment management layer for both government and commercial clients

Palantir's business model is centered around long-term, high-value software contracts, creating significant revenue visibility and stickiness. The company has aggressively pivoted toward AI/ML-driven commercial and government solutions, positioning itself at the intersection of national security technology and enterprise AI.
