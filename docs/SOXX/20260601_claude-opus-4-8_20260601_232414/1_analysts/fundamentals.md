## 1. 🏢 Company / Fund Profile

The **iShares Semiconductor ETF (SOXX)** is an exchange-traded fund managed by **BlackRock** under the iShares brand. It is listed on the **NASDAQ Global Market (NGM)** and is one of the most widely followed and traded semiconductor-sector ETFs in the U.S. equity market.

**Investment Objective:** SOXX seeks to track the investment results of the **ICE Semiconductor Index**, which is composed of U.S.-listed equities in the semiconductor sector. The ETF provides targeted exposure to companies involved in the design, manufacture, and distribution of semiconductors — an industry that underpins virtually all modern technology from AI accelerators and smartphones to automotive systems and data centers.

**Key Structural Characteristics:**
- **Asset Class:** Equity ETF
- **Sector Focus:** Semiconductors & Semiconductor Equipment
- **Geographic Focus:** Primarily U.S.-listed semiconductor companies (with international ADR exposure)
- **Fund Manager:** BlackRock / iShares
- **Benchmark:** ICE Semiconductor Index
- **Replication Method:** Physical (direct holdings in underlying securities)

**Typical Major Holdings (representative, subject to periodic rebalancing):** NVIDIA (NVDA), Broadcom (AVGO), QUALCOMM (QCOM), Intel (INTC), Texas Instruments (TXN), Lam Research (LRCX), Applied Materials (AMAT), KLA Corporation (KLAC), Marvell Technology (MRVL), and Micron Technology (MU) — among others. The index methodology is modified market-cap-weighted with single-stock caps to prevent excessive concentration.

- 📉 **Valuation Risk at 52.50x P/E:** Any earnings miss or guidance cut at a major constituent could trigger outsized ETF-level drawdowns.
- 🔄 **Cyclicality:** Semiconductors are inherently cyclical. Memory (DRAM/NAND) markets are prone to oversupply; a downturn in the PC or server refresh cycle could compress earnings rapidly.
- 💸 **Interest Rate Sensitivity:** High-growth, high-P/E equities remain sensitive to changes in long-term interest rates. If rates rise, discount rates increase and valuations compress.
- ⚡ **Geopolitical Risk (Taiwan):** TSMC manufactures the most advanced chips globally; any Taiwan Strait escalation would be catastrophic for the semiconductor supply chain and SOXX holdings.
