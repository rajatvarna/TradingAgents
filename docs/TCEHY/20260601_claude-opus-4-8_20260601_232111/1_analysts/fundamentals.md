## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Tencent Holdings Limited** (ticker: `TCEHY`) is one of the world's largest technology and internet conglomerates, headquartered in Shenzhen, China. The company operates across a diversified portfolio encompassing:

- **Social Networks & Messaging:** WeChat (Weixin), QQ — with over 1.3 billion combined monthly active users
- **Online Games:** Domestic and international gaming (owns Riot Games, Supercell stake, etc.)
- **FinTech & Business Services:** WeChat Pay, Cloud, Enterprise SaaS
- **Online Advertising:** Brand and performance advertising
- **Media & Entertainment:** Video streaming (Tencent Video), music (TME), literature, animation
- **Investment Portfolio:** Extensive stakes in global tech firms (Meituan, JD.com, Sea Ltd., Snap, etc.)

TCEHY trades as an OTC ADR (American Depositary Receipt) in the US, with the primary listing on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (0700.HK).
