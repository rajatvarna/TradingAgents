## 1. 🏢 Company Profile

**Tesla, Inc. (TSLA)** is the world's leading electric vehicle (EV) manufacturer and clean energy company, headquartered in Austin, Texas. Founded in 2003 and publicly listed on NASDAQ, Tesla designs, develops, manufactures, leases, and sells electric vehicles, energy generation systems (solar), and energy storage products (Powerwall, Megapack). Tesla also provides financial services and operates a global Supercharger network.

Tesla operates across two key business segments:
- **Automotive**: Vehicle sales (Model S, 3, X, Y, Cybertruck), leasing, regulatory credits, and Full Self-Driving (FSD) software
- **Energy Generation & Storage**: Solar panels, Powerwall, Megapack battery storage
- **Services & Other**: Non-warranty repairs, merchandise, auto insurance, and the Supercharger network
