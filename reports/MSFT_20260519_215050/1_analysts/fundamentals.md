## 8. 🔍 Key Risk Factors

1. **CapEx Overrun Risk:** The aggressive spend on AI infrastructure must eventually translate into proportional revenue. If AI monetization slows, FCF compression could become a concern.
2. **Regulatory Risks:** Ongoing antitrust scrutiny globally, especially in Europe and the US, around cloud and AI dominance.
3. **Competition:** Amazon AWS, Google Cloud, and emerging AI players represent persistent competitive threats in the cloud segment.
4. **Macro Sensitivity:** Enterprise IT spending is sensitive to economic slowdowns; a recession could delay cloud migration projects.
5. **Goodwill Concentration:** $119.7B in goodwill (mostly from Activision Blizzard acquisition) represents impairment risk if gaming performance disappoints.
